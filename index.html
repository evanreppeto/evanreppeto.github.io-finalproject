Welcome to HelpHub!

Here at HelpHub our aim is to find those who exploring opportunities in making a difference in the world. Our goal is to connect volunteers with local charities in their surrounding areas. With the simplicity of HelpHub it is that much easier to connect to volunteering opportunities.

If you are looking for opportunities in your area visit the Charities section at the top of the page.

If you are interested in learning more about particular charities visit the Charity Profile page which will be accessible after selecting the desired charity.

If you have any questions that need answering feel free to head to the Help page to contact us.
